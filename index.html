My Journey

My career began in the clinical heart of healthcare, working directly with patients in China. This direct exposure taught me the profound impact of medical systems on individual lives.

Driven to understand the larger mechanics of health, I pivoted to research and then into pharmaceutical strategy with Pfizer. In Market Access, I decoded the complex landscape of international pricing and regulatory frameworks.

Today, I am navigating the intersection of healthcare, data, and product. I believe that by applying product thinking to clinical needs and data insights, we can build a future of more accessible, efficient, and human-centric care.
